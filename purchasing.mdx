title: "Purchasing"
---

kanri is currently available in both free and kanri\+ versions.\

To purchasing kanri make sure you join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/eA6UgwMACk) and verify yourself\

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the server and community">
    Make sure you join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/eA6UgwMACk) and verify yourself in #verify then join our [Roblox Community](https://www.roblox.com/communities/911809461/kanri-community).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your account">
    After you have verified yourself join [kanrihub](https://www.roblox.com/games/121869931716981/kanri-hub) once you join you will be given a code `/link (code)`enter this code in #commands once you have linked you account click "Continue"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchase kanri">
    Once you have linked your account click on "Products" in the topbar and click on kanri once kanri is open click "Purchase"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the .rbxm file">
    Once kanri is purchased run `/recieve kanri`kanrihub should send you a dropbox link to the .rbxm file labeled "kanri download" click on that and download.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Purchasing kanri\+

<Steps>
  <Step title="Own kanri">
    To purchase kanri\+ ensure you already own kanri by following the steps above
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a ticket">
    Navigate to our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/eA6UgwMACk) and create a ticket in #support staff members should help you purchase kanri\+ as soon as possible
  </Step>
</Steps>
